## Description (4000 chars max)
ReadyTMS Driver is the mobile companion to the ReadyTMS dispatch platform. Built for over-the-road truckers and team drivers, it puts your loads, location sharing, and proof-of-delivery uploads in one focused, fast app.

**What you can do:**

• See every load assigned to you — pickup, delivery, broker, rate, weight, and commodity at a glance
• Advance load status with one tap — Picked Up, In Transit, Delivered
• Share GPS location with dispatch while On Duty — real background tracking that keeps working even when your phone is locked
• Snap a POD photo right from the camera and upload it instantly to the load record
• View your driver profile and account info

**Built for trucking:**

• Big tap targets for use with gloves
• Dark interface designed for night cab use
• Works on any unlocked phone with a cell signal
• Low data use — only loads and status changes go over the wire

**Privacy:**

ReadyTMS Driver only shares your location while you have the On Duty switch enabled. Toggle it off and location sharing stops immediately. Your fleet's dispatcher chooses what to do with the data.

**Requirements:**

This app is for drivers working with a carrier that uses ReadyTMS. If your dispatcher hasn't given you a login, the app won't be able to sign you in. Visit your carrier's office or web portal to set up your account first.

Need help? Contact your dispatcher or email support@readytms.com.
